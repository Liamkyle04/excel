Our Mission

Excel Driving Academy was established over 18 years ago in Dundalk, Co Louth. A quality assured independent driving school offering both manual and automatic lessons to complete beginners, intermediate learners, pretest candidates and advanced clients. We also facilitate lessons for clients who obtained their licence in another country and need to complete the Reduced EDT programme. And when it comes to sitting the test, we offer theory support, mock tests and car hire of both our top of the range Suzuki Swift and all electric Nissan leaf.

So whether you are starting your journey, needing a change of instructor, want to get test ready, would like some advice or just want to get more confident driving, Excel Driving Academy is only a phone call away.
